I grew up in the Bay Area with a hand-me-down film camera and a habit of pointing it at everyone I loved. Three decades later, not much has changed — I still think the best photographs are the ones where nobody's really thinking about being photographed.

My work leans cinematic and unhurried. I chase natural light, lean into shadow, and wait for the real moment instead of forcing the perfect one. Whether it's a quick headshot, a family on the coast at golden hour, or a full wedding day, my job is the same: pay close attention, stay out of the way, and hand you back something that still feels like that day years from now.

When I'm not behind a camera, I'm usually scouting back roads up Highway 1, over-ordering coffee, or printing photos far too large for the wall they're going on. If any of that sounds like your kind of thing, I'd love to make some pictures with you.

— Nguyendow ImagesPhotographer · California
How I work
