Good to know

Frequently asked

Do you travel? +

Yes — anywhere in California is fair game, and travel within 25 miles is included for most packages. The Half-Day Package includes 50 miles of travelling. For destinations beyond that, I'll add a simple travel fee to your quote.

How do I reserve a date? +

A 25% retainer hold your date. The retainer applies to your total and the balance is due the morning of the shoot.

When will I get my photos? +

Sessions are delivered within 7 days and weddings within 4–6 weeks. Sneak peeks go out within 48 hours so you have something to share right away.

Can we build a custom package? +

Absolutely. These are starting points. So tell me what you have in mind and I'll put together something that fits your day and budget.

Not sure which fits?

Tell me about your shoot and I'll point you to the right option — or build a custom one.
